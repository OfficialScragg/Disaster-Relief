Our Mission

Providing maritime-based disaster relief and sustainable support to coastal communities across Africa

Who We Are

Lifeline Global is a specialized humanitarian organization dedicated to providing maritime-based disaster relief and emergency response to coastal communities across Africa. Under the leadership of Managing Director Michael Taylor, we operate a state-of-the-art humanitarian relief vessel that serves as our primary platform for delivering aid.

Our vessel patrols the African coastline, responding to natural disasters, maritime emergencies, and humanitarian crises. We focus on remote coastal communities that are often inaccessible by traditional land-based relief efforts, ensuring that no community is left behind when disaster strikes.

How We Help

Comprehensive maritime disaster relief services for coastal communities in crisis

Maritime Emergency Response

Rapid response to shipwrecks, maritime accidents, and coastal emergencies with specialized rescue and medical teams.

Food & Water Distribution

Large-scale food and clean water distribution to coastal communities affected by disasters and food insecurity.

Medical Aid & Healthcare

Mobile medical clinics, emergency healthcare, vaccination programs, and medical supply distribution in coastal areas.

Shelter & Reconstruction

Emergency shelters, temporary housing, and coastal infrastructure reconstruction for disaster-affected communities.

Education & Training

Educational support, maritime safety training, and skills development programs for coastal communities.

Infrastructure Support

Coastal infrastructure restoration, clean water systems, and essential services reconstruction for sustainable recovery.
