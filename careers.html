Why Join Lifeline Global?

Make a meaningful impact while advancing your maritime career

Life-Changing Work

Every day, you'll be directly involved in saving lives and supporting communities in crisis. Our humanitarian relief vessel serves as a lifeline for coastal communities across Africa, providing emergency aid, medical care, and essential supplies when disaster strikes.

Under the leadership of Managing Director Michael Taylor, we've built a team of dedicated professionals who are committed to making a real difference in the world. Join us in our mission to bring hope and relief to those who need it most.
